Effective Note-Taking Strategies

January 15, 2025 9 min read

Taking notes properly can improve learning. Here are some methods:

  • Cornell Method: Divide the page into notes, cues, and summary sections.
  • Mind Mapping: Visual way to organize concepts.
  • Flow Notes: Best for conceptual subjects like physics.
  • Digital Notes: Use Notion, Evernote, or OneNote.
  • Review Regularly: Revise your notes weekly.
